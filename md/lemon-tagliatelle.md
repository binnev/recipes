# Lemon Tagliatelle

Author: Gennaro Contaldo

From: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SwDJi_PB-wY

Servings: 2

Preparation: 5 minutes

Cooking: 10 minutes

## Ingredients:
- [ ] 1 clove garlic, thinly sliced
- [ ] 1 red chilli, thinly sliced
- [ ] 1 lemon
- [ ] Tagliatelle
- [ ] Butter
- [ ] Parsley, finely chopped
- [ ] Pecorino

## Method:
1. Put the pasta on to cook
2. Sweat the garlic and chilli in some olive oil
3. Add the cooked pasta to the pan with some pasta water
4. Add a knob of butter
5. Squeeze in some lemon juice
6. Add the parsley
7. Grate over pecorino
