# Thai coconut chicken

Author: Nikittalouise

From: https://www.instagram.com/reel/DTN5i71jqDA/?igsh=Mzg1a3M3NWc2ZGc5

Servings: 4

## Ingredients:
- [ ] 500 g chicken mince
- [ ] 1 small courgette, grated
- [ ] 1 carrot, grated
- [ ] 2 spring onion, sliced
- [ ] 2 tsp garlic, minced
- [ ] 1 tsp ginger paste
- [ ] 1 tsp chilli paste
- [ ] 1 tbsp soy sauce
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] 1 lime, half the juice
- [ ] 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- [ ] 1 tsp garlic, minced
- [ ] 1 tsp ginger paste
- [ ] 2 tbsp soy sauce
- [ ] 400 ml coconut milk
- [ ] 2 tbsp red thai curry paste
- [ ] 2 spring onion, sliced
- [ ] Coriander, chopped
- [ ] 250 ml chicken stock
- [ ] 1 tbsp honey
- [ ] Rice

## Method:
1. Add the chicken mince, courgette, carrot, 2 spring onion, garlic, ginger paste, chilli paste, soy sauce, egg, lime and mix well together.
2. Split the mixture in 12 and form in meatballs.
3. Cook off in pan on a medium heat for 10 mins.
4. Remove from the pan & set to one side.
5. To the same pan add the peppers & fry off for 5 mins.
6. Add in the rest of the sauce ingredients, mix well and leave to simmer on a medium heat for 10 mins.
7. Add the meatballs back in to the pan, reduce the heat to low and allow to simmer for another 10 mins until the sauce has started to thicken.
8. Serve with rice & veg for a gorgeous warming winter meal!
