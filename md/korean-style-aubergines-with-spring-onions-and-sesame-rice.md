# Korean-style aubergines with spring onions and sesame rice

Author: Rukmini Iyer

From: The roasting tin around the world

Servings: 4

Preparation: 15 minutes

Cooking: 30 minutes

## Equipment: 
- Casserole dish or dutch oven pan

## Ingredients:
- [ ] 200 g basmati rice
- [ ] 2 cloves garlic
- [ ] 1 chinese cabbage, thinly sliced
- [ ] 400 ml vegetable stock
- [ ] 1 tbsp sesame oil
- [ ] 2 aubergines, cut into 1.5cm slices
- [ ] 1 tsp sea salt flakes
- [ ] 3 spring onions, very thinly sliced
- [ ] 1 tbsp sesame seeds
- [ ] 15 g gochugaru (or gochugang)
- [ ] 30 ml sesame oil
- [ ] 30 ml rice vinegar
- [ ] 30 ml soy sauce
- [ ] 5 cm ginger, grated
- [ ] 1 clove garlic, finely grated

## Method:
1. Preheat the oven to 210
2. Tip the rice and garlic into the casserole dish
3. Evenly cover with the chinese cabbage
4. Pour over the veg stock and sesame oil
5. Lay the aubergines over the top in 1 layer
6. Scatter over the sea salt, cover with the lid, and bake for 30 minutes
7. Mix the dressing ingredients
8. When it comes out of the oven, pour over the dressing and sprinkle over the spring onions and sesame seeds
